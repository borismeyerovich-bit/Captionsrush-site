Oren Lande

Founder & CEO

Oren Lande is the founder of CaptionsRush, dedicated to making competitive gaming accessible to players with hearing impairments. With a background in technology and a passion for inclusive design, Oren recognized the significant gap in accessibility support within the gaming industry.

Drawing from extensive experience in software development and user experience design, Oren has built a solution that bridges the accessibility divide in modern multiplayer gaming. His work focuses on creating technology that enhances gameplay for hearing-impaired gamers without compromising competitive integrity or game immersion.

Through CaptionsRush, Oren aims to set a new standard for accessibility in the gaming industry, demonstrating that inclusive design and competitive gameplay can coexist.

Why Accessibility Matters Now

The gaming industry has grown exponentially, becoming one of the largest entertainment sectors in the world. Yet, accessibility has often been an afterthought, leaving millions of players unable to fully participate in competitive gaming experiences.

Modern multiplayer games rely heavily on audio cues that exclude hearing-impaired players from competitive gameplay. This isn't just a design oversight—it's a fundamental barrier that prevents talented players from reaching their potential and enjoying the games they love.

We believe that accessibility and competitive excellence are not mutually exclusive. Our mission is to prove that with the right technology and design approach, we can create gaming experiences that are both inclusive and competitive, setting a new standard for the industry.

The time for accessible gaming is now. Every player deserves a level playing field.

Our Vision

For Players

A gaming experience where hearing ability doesn't determine competitive potential. Where every player has access to the same tactical information, regardless of their hearing status.

For Studios

Partnering with game studios to integrate accessibility from the ground up. Creating tools and solutions that enhance player experience while maintaining competitive integrity and game immersion.

For the Industry

Setting a new standard for accessibility in gaming. Demonstrating that inclusive design can be a competitive advantage and a source of innovation.

For the Future

Building a gaming ecosystem where accessibility is not an exception, but a fundamental principle of game design. Where every new game is designed with inclusivity in mind from day one.
