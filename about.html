About Equal Gaming

Our mission started with one simple belief: everyone deserves to play.

Oren Lande

Founder

Equal Gaming was founded by Oren Lande, a lifelong gamer and accessibility advocate who grew up seeing how many players are excluded from the gaming experience—especially real-time audio communication.

Inspired by conversations with deaf and hard-of-hearing players, he created CaptionsRush under the broader Equal Gaming platform to build the first dedicated gaming accessibility layer.

We're building tools that let more players join the action—and stay in the game.

Our Mission

Millions of players miss out because fast voice communication, visual cues, and complex controls aren't accessible enough. Studios want to help, but the tools they need don't exist yet.

We're building the accessibility layer for modern gaming—starting with CaptionsRush, our real-time voice-to-text solution for multiplayer games.

Gaming should be equal for everyone. We build accessibility tools that let more people play, connect, and compete.

Our Vision

For Players

A gaming experience where hearing ability doesn't determine competitive potential. Where every player has access to the same tactical information, regardless of their hearing status.

For Studios

Partnering with game studios to integrate accessibility from the ground up. Creating tools and solutions that enhance player experience while maintaining competitive integrity and game immersion.

For the Industry

Setting a new standard for accessibility in gaming. Demonstrating that inclusive design can be a competitive advantage and a source of innovation.

For the Future

Building a gaming ecosystem where accessibility is not an exception, but a fundamental principle of game design. Where every new game is designed with inclusivity in mind from day one.
