The Problem

Modern multiplayer games rely heavily on directional audio, voice callouts, and subtle sound cues—footsteps, reloads, explosions, proximity alerts.

For millions of hearing-impaired gamers, these cues are inaccessible, creating:

  • • Slower reaction times
  • • Poorer team communication
  • • Higher exclusion rates from squads
  • • Misaligned competitive fairness

Our Solution

A real-time system that translates important sound events into non-intrusive visual cues. Our technology:

Interpretation

Visual Output
