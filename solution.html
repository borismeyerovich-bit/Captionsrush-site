CaptionsRush

Real-time captions built for gaming. Lets deaf and hard-of-hearing players fully join multiplayer action.

Product Overview

CaptionsRush is our first product under the Equal Gaming platform—a real-time voice-to-text solution specifically built for multiplayer gaming. It provides real-time captions with under 200ms latency, high accuracy even in chaotic audio environments, speaker labeling, and adjustable overlays.

With low overhead and Unity + Unreal SDK support coming soon, CaptionsRush lets deaf and hard-of-hearing players fully join multiplayer action without compromising game performance or competitive balance.

Key Features

Under 200ms Latency

Near-instantaneous caption delivery ensures players never miss critical communication.

High Accuracy

Reliable transcription even in chaotic audio environments with multiple speakers and game sounds.

Speaker Labeling

Identify who's speaking to improve team coordination and communication clarity.

Adjustable Overlays

Customize position and appearance to match your gaming setup and preferences.

Low Overhead

Minimal performance impact ensures smooth gameplay without frame drops or lag.

Unity + Unreal SDK

Easy integration with popular game engines. Coming soon.
