Our Solution

Real-time visual cues that translate audio events into accessible, non-intrusive on-screen indicators.

System Overview

Our solution is a real-time system that translates important sound events into non-intrusive visual cues. The technology detects critical audio events, classifies and prioritizes them, and displays simple on-screen indicators that enhance accessibility while maintaining game immersion and competitive balance.

The system is designed to keep immersion intact and avoid competitive imbalance, ensuring that all players have equal access to critical gameplay information regardless of their hearing ability.

How It Works

Detection

Our system continuously monitors game audio in real-time, detecting critical sound events as they occur.

  • • Footsteps and movement cues
  • • Weapon sounds and reloads
  • • Explosions and alerts
  • • Voice callouts and announcements

Interpretation

Advanced classification algorithms analyze and prioritize detected events, filtering out noise and identifying what matters most.

  • • Event classification
  • • Priority ranking
  • • Contextual understanding
  • • Noise filtering

Visual Output

Simple, clear visual indicators appear on-screen, providing the same tactical information that audio cues deliver.

  • • Non-intrusive design
  • • Directional indicators
  • • Event type visualization
  • • Customizable positioning
